About Lyles Doberman

Lyles European Dobermans is a small family business that is located in the suburbs of Chicago. We have been training Dobermans since 1978. In the mid 80’s we also showed, breed, raised American Pit Bull Terriers and American Staffordshire Terriers. We also started a dog club with the United Kennel Club on the island of Oahu. In 2006 we went back to our first love “The European Doberman”.


Our dobermans have been hand selected and imported from Europe. Our breeding raising and training process reflects our desire of producing the ultimate family guardian for your family’s protection, security and safety. We offer show and working quality puppies for sale. We only have one or two litters a year. The Superior Genetics of our imported Dobermans produce stable, balanced and correct Dobermans for both work or show.
